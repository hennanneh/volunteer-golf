Volunteer Golf Help Guide

Everything you need to manage tournament volunteers

Quick Start: Checking In Volunteers

Get your hole checked in in under 60 seconds:

  1. Open the app on your phone and tap "Hole Captain Check-In"
  2. Enter your PIN (last 4 digits of your phone, or your custom PIN)
  3. Select AM or PM shift using the toggle at the top
  4. Tap each volunteer's name as they arrive - a green checkmark appears
  5. Tap "Save Check-Ins" when done - that's it!
Pro Tip: You can check in volunteers throughout the shift. No need to wait until everyone arrives - just save whenever you make changes.

User Roles

Hole Captain

Checks in volunteers at their assigned hole. Can view their hole's roster and email volunteers.

Chairman / Asst. Chairman

Like a captain but can switch between multiple assigned holes. Oversees a section of the course.

Admin

Full access to all features: edit volunteers, view all holes, run reports, manage settings.

View Admin

Read-only admin access. Can view all data and reports but cannot make changes.

Volunteer

Can view their own schedule and check-in status via the Volunteer Portal.

Admin Features

Overview Tab

Your command center showing real-time tournament status:

  • Week at a Glance - Visual grid of all shifts showing scheduled vs checked-in counts
  • Color coding - Green = fully staffed, Yellow = partial, Gray = no data yet
  • Click any cell to drill down into that day/shift's details

Check-ins Tab

Real-time view of all check-in activity:

  • See who checked in, when, and which captain recorded it
  • Filter by day, shift, or hole
  • Monitor progress across the tournament

Edit Volunteers Tab

Manage your volunteer roster:

  • Search & Filter - Find volunteers by name, type, or hole
  • Add Volunteer - Click the + button to add new volunteers
  • Edit - Click any volunteer to update their info, schedule, or PIN
  • Bulk Update - Use Import to update multiple volunteers at once
Tip: Volunteer types are Captain, Asst. Chairman, Chairman, Admin, or Volunteer. Only Captain+ roles can check in others.

Reports Tab

Generate insights and export data:

ReportDescription
Volunteer TotalsTotal shifts scheduled and worked per volunteer
Hole RosterComplete schedule for a specific hole - great for printing
Custom ReportBuild your own with selectable columns and filters

All reports can be printed or exported to CSV for Excel.

Import Tab

Bulk import or update volunteers from a spreadsheet:

  1. Prepare a CSV file with columns: Name, Phone, Email, Type, Hole, Schedule
  2. Upload the file - the system auto-maps columns
  3. Review the preview and click Import
Tip: You can re-import to update existing volunteers. The system matches by name.

Captain Features

Check-In Screen

  • AM/PM Toggle - Switch between morning and afternoon shifts
  • Volunteer List - Shows all volunteers scheduled for your hole and current shift
  • Tap to Check In - Tap a name to toggle their check-in status
  • Progress Bar - Shows how many are checked in vs scheduled
  • Save Check-Ins - Saves your changes to the server

Roster View

Tap the "Roster" button to see your full hole roster for all days/shifts. You can also email the roster to all volunteers with email addresses on file.

Custom PIN

On first login, you'll be prompted to set a custom 4-digit PIN. This replaces using your phone number and is more secure.

Note: Easy PINs like 1234, 1111, or 0000 are not allowed.

Volunteer Portal

Volunteers can log in with the last 4 digits of their phone to:

  • View their scheduled shifts
  • See which shifts they've been checked into
  • Update their email address
  • View their hole assignment

Real-Time Updates

The app uses WebSockets for instant synchronization:

  • When a captain checks in volunteers, admins see updates immediately
  • Multiple captains on the same hole see each other's changes
  • No need to refresh - data syncs automatically
  • A toast notification appears when data is updated by another user

Install as App

Add this app to your phone's home screen for the best experience:

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open the site in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button (square with arrow)
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add

Android

  1. Open the site in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu (three dots)
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
Benefit: The app will launch full-screen, remember your login, and feel like a native app.

Pull-to-Refresh

On mobile devices, you can refresh data by pulling down from the top of the screen:

  • Swipe down from the top when at the top of the page
  • A refresh indicator will appear
  • Release to refresh data from the server

You can also tap the refresh button (↻) in the header on any screen.

Hat Pickup

Track volunteer hat distribution using QR codes:

  • In Admin > Reports, generate QR codes for hat pickup
  • Each volunteer has a unique QR code linking to their hat info
  • When distributing hats, scan the QR code and tap "Mark as Delivered"
  • The system tracks which hats have been picked up
Tip: Print the QR codes or display them on a tablet for quick scanning during hat distribution.

Demo Mode

Add ?demo=true to the URL to enter demo mode:

  • Uses separate demo data that won't affect real data
  • Data resets every hour automatically
  • Perfect for training or showing prospects
  • Exit by clicking the demo banner or adding ?demo=false

Tips & Best Practices

  • Import early - Get your volunteer list imported a few days before the event
  • Test with captains - Have captains log in before event day to set their PINs
  • Use the Overview - The admin Overview tab is your best friend during the tournament
  • Email rosters - Send hole rosters to volunteers before their shift
  • Archive when done - Save the tournament data to archives after the event

Troubleshooting

ProblemSolution
Can't log inMake sure you're using the last 4 digits of your registered phone, or your custom PIN if you set one
Don't see my volunteersCheck the AM/PM toggle - you might be viewing the wrong shift
Changes not savingCheck your internet connection. The app needs connectivity to save.
Seeing old dataPull down to refresh (mobile) or tap the refresh button in the header
Logged out unexpectedlySessions are saved locally - if you cleared browser data, you'll need to log in again
App not updatingIf using the installed app, try closing and reopening it, or clear the app cache
