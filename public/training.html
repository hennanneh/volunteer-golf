📝 Try It Yourself!

Practice logging in and checking in volunteers

Task 1: Log In and Check In

  1. Open colonialvolunteers.golf on your phone
  2. Tap "Hole Captain Check-In"
  3. Enter your email and password (first time: 10-digit phone number)
  4. Verify the correct day and shift at the top
  5. Find yourself in the Captains section (look for "You" badge)
  6. Check in one volunteer by tapping their row
  7. Uncheck them by tapping again
  8. Try the Search bar to find a specific name
💡 Your check-ins save automatically and persist even if you close the app!

📝 Try It Yourself!

Practice the full captain workflow

Task 2: Complete Captain Workflow

  1. Tap Menu → Roster to view your hole's schedule
  2. Close the roster by tapping X
  3. Tap "Edit" next to any volunteer
  4. Look at their schedule checkboxes (don't change anything)
  5. Tap "Cancel"
  6. Check in 2-3 volunteers by tapping their rows
  7. Scroll down and type a note: "Testing the system"
  8. Tap the Submit button and confirm
  9. Notice the button now says "No Changes"
💡 After submitting, the button updates to show only new changes since the last submission.

Quick Reference: Captains

✅ DO

  • Check in volunteers as they arrive
  • Verify the correct day and shift first
  • Submit your shift report with notes
  • Add notes about no-shows or issues
  • Use Edit to fix schedule mistakes
  • Tap Menu → Help if you need guidance
  • Download the Roster for reference

❌ DON'T

  • Check in people who didn't actually show up
  • Forget to verify the day/shift selector
  • Worry about mistakes - just tap again to uncheck!
  • Wait until shift end to check everyone in
  • Edit schedules without being sure of changes

💡 You can submit multiple times. Late arrival? Check them in and submit again!

Troubleshooting

🔐

"Login Failed"

Check your email spelling. First-time password is your 10-digit phone number (no dashes or spaces).

🚫

"This shift is closed"

Check-ins lock after shift cutoff time. Contact tournament staff for changes.

👤

Can't find my name

Make sure you're in the right portal (Captain vs Volunteer) and the correct day/shift is selected.

🔄

Page stuck or not loading

Tap the refresh button (↻) or pull down to refresh. Check your internet connection.

📱

Buttons too small

Use your phone's zoom feature, or try landscape orientation.

🔑

Forgot my password

Contact tournament staff to reset your password to your phone number.
